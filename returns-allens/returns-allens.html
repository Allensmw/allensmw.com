Returns Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy for most items.

How to Return an Item:

  1. Log into your account and go to your order history.
  2. Select the item you wish to return and choose a reason for the return.
  3. Print the prepaid return label provided.
  4. Pack the item securely in its original packaging if possible.
  5. Attach the return label to your package.
  6. Drop off the package at your nearest post office or schedule a pickup.

Return Conditions:

  • Items must be unused, unworn, and in the same condition as received.
  • Items must be in the original packaging.
  • Sale items are final and cannot be returned unless defective.
  • Certain items, such as intimates and personalized products, are not eligible for return.

Once we receive your return, please allow 5-7 business days for processing. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

If you have any questions about our return policy, please contact our customer service team.
